About Me

Hi, I'm Shelby! I'm a Turing Scholar at The University of Texas at Austin, working towards my B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in Economics.

I have experience with Java, Python, C++, C, Verilog, SQL, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript.

When I'm not coding, you can find me gaming, taking photos, writing blogs, and exploring new technologies. I'm passionate about machine learning and it's applications in economics.

I've developed a stand-alone web crawling application, a 5-stage pipelined CPU in Verilog, and I also enjoy creating games like Tetris and Boggle.

Feel free to reach out to me for any potential opportunities or just to say hi!
